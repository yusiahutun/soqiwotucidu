Your Money Horoscope 2024 For Each Zodiac Sign Is Here

Your 2024 money horoscope for each zodiac sign implies that abundance will be possible, but only if you are flexible and open-minded. Between inflation and the strained job market, the financial astrology of 2023 most certainly felt bleak at times. Although this might be hard to believe, the financial astrology of 2024 seems to be more promising than anticipated. The stars will align and bring forth multiple opportunities for financial growth.

The start of the show will be Jupiter! In astrology, Jupiter rules over luck, abundance, and growth. This happy-go-lucky planet is kind of like the King Midas of astrology since everything Jupiter touches turns to gold. No matter the Jupiter transit, you can always use Jupiter’s serendipity to turn your luck around. 

From January 1, 2024, to May 25, 2024, the planet of luck will be in fiscally-focused Taurus. During this time, Jupiter will expand upon the Taurus themes of personal wealth, materialism, and value systems. Long-awaited opportunities aligned with your financial goals and professional values may fall into place during Jupiter in Taurus. You could even sell old items to buy higher quality replacements that will stand the test of time. The only potential downside to Jupiter in Taurus will be over-indulging by overspending. 

By May 25, 2024, a new financial trend will arise since Jupiter will enter Gemini and be in the sign of the twins for the remainder of 2024. According to traditional astrology, Jupiter in Gemini might present more obstacles and challenges. But don’t let this get you down! Jupiter in Gemini will encourage you to become more financially literate. You might benefit from reading more on financial outlets, speaking to a financial advisor, or listening to economic podcasts. Since the keyword is “multiple”, the key to Jupiter in Gemini will be investing in several accounts. You could have a few stock portfolios, a 401K, a Roth IRA, a high-yield savings account, and more. Not to mention that most individuals may even multi-task several jobs, like a regular nine-to-five and at least one side hustle.

Luck may turn on a dime when Jupiter stations retrograde in Gemini from October 9, 2024, to February 4, 2025. Whatever you worked on over the last few months may hit a wall. You could find fewer accessible resources on financial literacy, so you may not learn anything new. Side hustles might become far and few in between. Even your consistent, seemingly stable side hustles might not have as many hours or assignments as before. There might be some professional and financial instability, you may have to take some money out of your savings. Not to mention that your stocks could either lose value or stagnate, so you may not see a substantial difference during Jupiter retrograde in Gemini. The best way to handle this retrograde is to have a semi-flexible plan, which may include cutting down on frivolous pastimes and becoming more creative to make money.

Lastly, there might be one transit that could go either way in 2024, and that is Uranus in Taurus. According to astrology, Uranus is the planet of radical change and innovation, so this is a challenging planet to track for financial trends. Regardless, there might be a sudden change of pace when Uranus stations direct in Taurus on January 27, 2024. Once direct, you may entertain unique financial possibilities over the following months. Drastic measures could be taken to increase your means for financial stability and security. However, there might be fewer twists and turns when Uranus stations retrograde in Taurus from September 1, 2024, to January 31, 2025. During the remainder of 2024, Uranus retrograde may encourage you to be more reflective about what you value for your financial avenues.
