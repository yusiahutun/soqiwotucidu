Russell Simmons has helped launch the careers of some of the biggest musicians on the planet through Def Jam Recordings - but his successes have been overshadowed by a slew of controversies, horrific allegations of abuse and sexual assault, and his bitter divorce from Kimora Lee Simons.

His romantic past has now been dredged up again when the music mogul spoke out to vehemently deny allegations of sexual assault made by multiple women back in 2017. 

While he furiously shut down the claims, Russell confessed to having had a very varied love life, saying in a new interview that he'd 'slept with thousands of women'.  

The mogul, now 66, became a major figure in the music industry after he co-founded the record label in 1984, but as he rose to the top, he quickly became known for being an ardent womanizer.

Russell's bad boy reputation didn't deter Kimora when she met him as a 17-year-old starry-eyed rising model - and despite her friends warning her that Russell, who was 35 at the time, was someone she should steer clear of, she fell quickly for the music executive.

The romance was scandalous from the start - she was still a high school student and he was 18 years her senior - and the immense age gap, as well as Russell's flirtatious behavior, did not make for a great foundation for the love affair.

She told Russell that he would have to prove his loyalty once and for all by marrying her or that she was done - and he shocked her (and the rest of the world) by choosing the first option.
