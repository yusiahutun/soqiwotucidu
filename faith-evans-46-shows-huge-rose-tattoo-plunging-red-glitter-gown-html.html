Daring: The hip-hop singer, 46, wowed in a daring red gown which showed off her curves on the celeb-packed red carpet

The singer's outing comes shortly after she condemned a forthcoming Lifetime documentary about her 'intense and tragic' relationship with the Notorious BIG.

Titled Biggie and Faith Evans, the documentary explores the singer's life with her late husband Christopher Wallace, aka the rapper Notorious BIG, who was killed in a drive-by shooting in 1997.

Faith insists she was duped into contributing to the Lifetime programme, telling Page Six that she had been told the documentary would be a 'positive piece'.

PDA: Faith was joined at the party, which this year was held in honor of Diddy and his 25-year trailblazing career, by her producer husband Stevie J

'I feel upset that I'm attached to it,' she told Page Six. 'I was told that it would be a positive piece with a little more of my personal background.'

'It wasn't pitched to me as a story rehashing the sensationalised events that took place.

'I am beyond that and have no need to open old wounds, damage repaired relationships and, above all, soil the legacy of Christopher Wallace aka the Notorious BIG.'
