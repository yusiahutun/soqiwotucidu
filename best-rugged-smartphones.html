FAQ

Q: What Does Rugged Phone Mean?

A rugged phone has been purposely designed to withstand damage from various sources, such as water, shock, dust, and vibration, among others.

Rugged phones are ideal for those that often do outdoor activities, extreme sports, or work in hazardous roles.

Q: What Phone Is Unbreakable?

No phone is truly unbreakable, however, there are several high-quality components that can be added to ensure a much more durable device and reduce the chances of your rugged phone being broken.

Many rugged phones will have an IP68 rating and will use corning gorilla glass to make sure that the screen is less likely to become damaged. There are also several design options many manufacturers choose to reduce the chances of damage.

Q: Can Gorilla Glass Be Broken?

Gorilla glass can break, but unlike normal glass, it has been designed to withstand everyday life, and should even be able to survive everyday drops.

As long as you don't go out of your way to try and break the glass, Gorilla glass should be more than enough to ensure your screen remains undamaged.
