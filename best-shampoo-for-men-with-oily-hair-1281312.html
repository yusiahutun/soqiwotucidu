    How to Select the Best Men's Shampoo for Oily Hair?

    Here are a few aspects that you should consider before splurging on the best men's shampoo for oily hair: 

    Hair and Scalp Type: Make sure you analyze your hair type before investing in any of the men's shampoos for oily scalp. For those with dry, oily, and itchy scalps, get your hands on clarifying shampoos that remove buildup, impurities, and flakes, and leave your scalp itch-free. Likewise, a gentle and herbal shampoo is a great pick for someone who is dealing with sensitive scalp and brittle hair. If your oily scalp is prone to dandruff, then vouch for an anti-dandruff shampoo with great cleansing properties that leave hair soft and smooth. 

    Ingredients: Always pay heed to the list of ingredients. Invest in a men's shampoo for oily hair that is enriched with natural ingredients like rosemary leaf extracts, jojoba seed oil, tea tree, argan oil, aloe vera, chamomile, botanical extracts, and plant proteins. Refrain from purchasing shampoos that contain sulfates, silicones, phthalates, gluten, and the rest of the harsh chemicals. 

    Formula: The next aspect to consider is the formula of the shampoo that you are planning to buy. The shampoo should control oil and sebum production but at the same time, it should not strip away the natural moisture and oil off your shafts and hair follicles. This can lead to potential hair damage, breakage, hair loss, and dryness. 

    Clarifying Properties: Ensure that you consider splurging on shampoos with a plethora of clarifying and hair-nourishing properties. The shampoo should help remove dirt, oil, and product residue, leaving your hair feeling fresh and revitalized. 

    After taking note of the above-mentioned aspects, get to know the right way to use shampoo for oily hair for men.

    Steps to Apply Shampoo for Oily Hair

    Below are the steps to apply men's shampoo for oily hair accurately:  

    Step 1: Wet your hair and focus more on cleansing your scalp.

    Step 2: Pour a sufficient amount of shampoo. If it is too strong, then consider diluting it a little with water. 

    Step 3: Begin applying the shampoo onto your scalp and massage it with your fingertips. Do not scrub vigorously as it will lead to hair breakage.

    Step 4: Rinse thoroughly. 

    Step 5: Squeeze your hair a little and apply a conditioner. Focus more on the ends of your hair strands. Do not apply it on your scalp. The conditioner will help in improving the look of damaged or weathered hair. 

    Step 6: Rinse thoroughly, damp it with a microfiber towel, and allow your hair to dry naturally.  

    Tada, you are done!

    FAQ's

    Is it ok for men to shampoo oily scalp every day?

    Yes, it is ok for men to shampoo oily scalp every day. However, it is recommended to use a mild shampoo suitable for daily use as over-stripping the scalp of natural oils may increase sebum production in the long run.

    Can men’s oily hair cause hair loss?

    Yes, excess production of sebum can lead to blockage, inflammation, and hardening of hair follicles, thereby causing hair loss.

    How do I know if I have oily hair?

    Oily hair leaves a thin layer on the scalp making it feel greasy to the touch. If you witness the same, it means you have oily hair.

    How often should I wash my hair if it's oily?

    Depending on whether your hair is thin or thick, you can wash your oily hair between 3 to 4 days a week to avoid greasiness. However, some prefer to wash every day using a daily use shampoo like American Crew’s Daily Cleansing Shampoo.

    How does oily hair differ from other hair types?

    Oily hair tends to look and feel more greasy than other hair types.

    How long does it take to see results after using an oily shampoo?

    It takes at least one to two weeks to see results after using a new shampoo.

    Can one use dry shampoo between washes to manage oily hair?

    Yes, you can use dry shampoo between washes to manage oily hair and combat greasiness and odor.
