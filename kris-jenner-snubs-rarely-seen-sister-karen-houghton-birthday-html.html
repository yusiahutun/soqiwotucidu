Kris Jenner 'snubs rarely-seen sister Karen Houghton' on birthday

They're thought to have had a fractitious relationship over the years.

The momager - who has children Kourtney, 39, Kim, 38, Khloe, 34, and Rob Kardashian, 31, with her late husband Robert, and Kendall, 23, and Kylie Jenner, 21, with ex-spouse Bruce - failed to publicly acknowledge the landmark birthday, despite regularly sharing posts supporting her family and friends.
